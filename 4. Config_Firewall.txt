IPTABLES

1. Connect to VM with username
2. Add "iptables" in /etc/network/if-pre-up.d/iptables
3. sudo chmod -x /etc/network/if-pre-up.d/iptables
4. sudo sh /etc/network/if-pre-up.d/iptables
